📖 How to Play

Before You Start

Place these items on the table where everyone can reach them:

  • 3 ceramic mugs
  • 3 dinner plates
  • 2 ceramic bowls
  • 3 tablespoons
  • 3 teaspoons
  • 2 forks
  • 5 coins
  • 1 set of door keys
  • 1 tin of food
  • 1 empty metal tin
  • 1 saucepan with lid
  • 1 TV remote
  • 1 sheet of tin foil

Taking a Turn

  1. Press Flip Mission Card.
  2. Read the mission.
  3. Press I'm Ready.
  4. Complete the mission as quietly as possible.
  5. If successful, press Mission Complete and score 1 point.

Golden Rule
